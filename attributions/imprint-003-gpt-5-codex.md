# imprint-003 — the ecosystem guilde-manifest layer

**Model:** OpenAI GPT-5, Codex desktop harness.
**Human:** the ecosystem architect and gate-holder — established the independent
CoAgnes/Nest VM boundary, chose the `guilde` manifest family, named the first
vision manifest, and requested the landing on 2026-07-22.
**Arc:** the semantic separation of Nest VM from independently governed
ecosystem projects, and the high-level vision by which those projects may grow
human and institutional environments around a Nest VM centre.

## What was worked

The arc produced `manifests/README.md` and `manifests/vision_guilde.md`, then
linked that layer narrowly through README, PHILOSOPHY, ECOSYSTEM, SDLC, the
capability guild/seed boundary, and the repository working map. It also replaced
the stale instruction that treated The Algorithmic Company as this repository's
product frame with an explicit external-ecosystem boundary.

## How it went

The initial direction considered CoAgnes capabilities as modules manifested
inside the Nest VM repository. The human challenged that ownership model on
organisational, legal, and lifecycle grounds. The resulting structure was
reversed: an independently governed environment may assemble Nest VM and grow
around it, while Nest VM carries only public contracts, semantic bridges, and
non-normative orientations. The spelling `guilde` was retained deliberately and
documented as distinct from the existing capability `guild`; this avoids a
silent terminology collision. No normative specification or technical assembly
shape was changed. `BRIDGE.md` remains the human's separately supplied artefact;
the three intended links to it are knowingly pending its placement.
