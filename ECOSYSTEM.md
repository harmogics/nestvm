# The Harmogics ecosystem around Nest VM

**Status:** public orientation. This document names responsibilities and flows
between projects. It does not promote SEED or related directions into the Nest
VM normative contract.

Nest VM lives inside the wider Harmogics field. The projects share a concern
with how thought becomes movement, how movement becomes inspectable experience,
and how useful experience becomes a reusable figure. They do not collapse into
one programme or one storage system.

## The parts of the field

| Part | Responsibility | Present status in this repository |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Harmogics** | The public field and commons in which figures, methods, examples, and reviewed structural derivatives can be published, compared, and developed. | Public frame |
| **Nest VM** | The specification-defined machine that executes declared figures, records their movement in a wave log, and makes their histories readable. | CURRENT specification; simulated workbench |
| **Nest Studio** | A workbench shell over Nest VM concepts: today it presents the specification and lets a person study it through Nest-shaped session histories. | Experimental product surface |
| **HUID** | The Human Interaction Device specification: a stable host, feed, parameter space, docks, and module contract for building shells without making each panel a new mechanism. | SEED device layer |
| **CoAgnes** | The organisational experience direction: evidence-backed descriptions, professional gestures, guild and company libraries, trials, assessment, and reviewed promotion. | SEED extension direction |
| **Florispace** | The related context-formation direction: graph-based selection and assembly of the material a knot or figure needs, including weighted relationships and alternative context algorithms. | Related design direction; not a Nest VM contract here |
| **The Algorithmic Company** | An application frame for making employee, process, and organisational capability articulable and teachable through the same field. | Product and design frame |

The status column matters. A shared vocabulary or source lineage is not enough
to turn a direction into implemented machine capability.

## Nest VM's place

Nest VM is the execution and recording substrate of the field. It says how a
figure is declared, how facts move, how knots accumulate, how binds gather and
publish, how the membrane crosses into the world, and how a run settles.

It does not decide which organisational practice is valuable, which public
figure deserves promotion, or which context-selection strategy is correct.
Those are responsibilities of packages, product policy, assessment, review,
and the surrounding ecosystem.

The public Nest VM site may therefore be hosted as `nestvm.harmogics.com`
while remaining a distinct specification and workbench. Being part of
Harmogics supplies its public context; it does not weaken its conformance
boundary.

## Harmogics as a commons of figures

The primary public unit is not a source-code fork and not a raw conversation.
It is a versioned figure package: declared movement, schemas, fixtures,
applicability, evaluation, provenance, and licence.

A figure may express, for example:

- how to unfold an unfamiliar domain into answerable obligations;
- how to compare conflicting evidence without laundering the seam;
- how to move from an employee's articulation to a reviewed process norm;
- how to assess whether an apparently complete result remains unsupported;
- how to assemble and test a new component proposed from observed work.

Harmogics lets such figures be studied as figures: compared across contexts,
adapted, tried, criticised, and recomposed. The Nest VM authoring formats give
them a portable executable centre.

## The two directions of flow

### From the commons into work

```text
Harmogics figure
  → local package review and admission
  → Nest VM implementation and assembly
  → domain run
  → attributable local wave history
  → derived readings and human judgement
```

A public figure does not bypass local policy. It enters a fresh assembly with
declared controllers, budgets, data boundaries, and a result contract.

### From work back into the commons

```text
private attributed history
  → local structural analysis
  → proposed reusable figure or trace derivative
  → de-identification and rights review
  → trial, assessment, and human decision
  → versioned public Harmogics artefact
```

This is a donation threshold, not telemetry. The public artefact is intentionally
formed from experience; it is not the accidental exposure of experience.

## CoAgnes: experience inside organisations

CoAgnes concerns the level at which gestures belong to professions, guilds,
teams, and companies. Its source material may include explanations, decisions,
exceptions, evidence, competing practices, and the paths by which experienced
people recognise what to do.

Nest VM can execute and record figures used to elicit, teach, test, and revise
that experience. CoAgnes adds the organisational contracts: tenancy, roles,
review authority, attribution, evaluation independence, promotion, retention,
and transfer between personal, group, and organisational layers.

Personal attribution and public anonymity are not opposites. An organisation
may preserve precise internal authorship while publishing only a reviewed,
generalised structure. The transition between the two is explicit and
auditable.

## Florispace: forming the context of a movement

The Florispace direction concerns how relevant material is drawn towards a
figure: graph neighbourhoods, weighted relations, semantic proximity,
constraints, evidence types, and alternative algorithms for assembling a
bounded context.

This complements Nest VM without becoming a second causal truth. Florispace may
propose or resolve a context manifest; Nest VM records which manifest and
references a run actually used. Selection remains distinguishable from
execution, and a changed context produces a new attributable act rather than a
retroactive change to history.

The broader Florispace role remains a related direction until its own contracts
are grounded and published. This repository does not infer a normative system
from source lineage alone.

## HUID: one machine, many readable surfaces

HUID carries the same discipline to interaction. Modules read declared parts of
the feed, derive views, and shape declared gestures. They do not call one
another, infer semantic results in the browser, or acquire undeclared access to
the machine.

This allows Nest Studio, organisational workbenches, public figure explorers,
and future specialised devices to share a motherboard without becoming the same
product.

## Boundaries that keep the ecosystem legible

1. **Specification is not implementation.** A compatible implementation is
   recognised by conformance, not ancestry.
2. **History is not publication.** Recording a session grants no automatic
   permission to expose or reuse it.
3. **Precedent is not a norm.** A successful figure remains contextual until a
   separate review promotes it for a declared scope.
4. **Context selection is not execution.** A graph or model may assemble what a
   figure sees; the wave log records what was actually presented and done.
5. **A surface is not a controller.** HUID reads and commits through declared
   ports; external capability belongs to the membrane and assembly.
6. **Shared language is not shared authority.** Harmogics, Nest VM, CoAgnes,
   Florispace, and their products may inform one another without silently
   changing one another's contracts.

The ecosystem is therefore compositional: one field of inquiry, several
responsibilities, portable figures between them, and explicit thresholds where
experience changes scope.
